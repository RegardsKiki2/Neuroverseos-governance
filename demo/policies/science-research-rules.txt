Scientific Research Integrity Policy

Require at least 2 peer-reviewed sources before analysis can proceed
Require confidence level above 0.7 for publication
Require cross-referencing of at least 3 independent sources
Require uncertainty language when confidence is below 0.9
Monitor for investigations with unfulfilled needs signals
Block publication when paper_count is 0
Block synthesis artifacts with zero citations or sources
Block claims that lack specific source citations
Block ml_prediction artifacts that use synthetic or unnamed features
